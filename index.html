What is Newton's second law of motion?

Newton's second law states that the acceleration of an object is directly proportional to the net force acting on it and inversely proportional to its mass. The formula is F = m × a, where F is force, m is mass, and a is acceleration.

Can you help me solve 3x - 7 = 14?

Math Agent Math Equation Solver

Solving: 3x - 7 = 14

Step 1: Start with the original equation

3x - 7 = 14

Step 2: Add 7 to both sides

3x = 14 + 7 → 3x = 21

This isolates the term with the variable on one side.

Step 3: Divide both sides by 3

x = 21 ÷ 3 → x = 7

Dividing both sides by the coefficient of x.

Solution: x = 7

Verification: Plugging back into original equation:

3(7) - 7 = 21 - 7 = 14

Both sides are equal! Solution is correct.
